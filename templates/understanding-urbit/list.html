Introduction

This is a drawing of an Urbit.

It’s a new kind of computer.

This image is representative of three key (yet familiar) aspects to this new kind of computer:

  • The apps you use every day,
  • the OS that makes sure everything runs smoothly,
  • and your identity at the center of it all.

So, what is an Urbit?

Today, technology controls us. Every MEGACORP has the same incentive: To keep us hooked on their platform.

We built Urbit so people can control their technology, shape their digital lives and freely build their digital communities.

We want to replace the internet with a foundation for computing that we can all rely on far into the future.

Urbit is a large, ambitious project. But at the core it’s durable, simple and yours.

Want to continue down this rabbit hole? If you’re new to Urbit and want to learn more, start from the beginning. If you’re technical, start with the technical overview.
