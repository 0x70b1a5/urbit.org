Urbit Grants Program

Urbit is a community project. While anyone can contribute, we help focus development and reward exceptional contribution through our grants and bounties program.

We reward contributors in four different ways:

  • Proposals: Initiated by community members
  • Bounties: Work on scoped areas put out for application
  • Scholarships: Advanced infrastructural projects undertaken with a mentor
  • Gifts: Given post facto to exceptional contributors. See previous gift recipients.

Contributors of all types have access to a wide variety of resources while working on projects, including a supportive team at urbit.org, Tlon developers, and community mentors.
